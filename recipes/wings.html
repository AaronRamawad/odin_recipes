Buffalo Wings

A picture of buffalo wings

These wings are able to fill the craving that you've had when you looked at a wingstop or buffalo wild wings. This buffalo wing recipe is easy to make, with litle prep time and simple steps. You get to enjoy some delicious wings that won't greatly impact your health in a negative way.

Ingredients

Chicken Wings

  • 4 pound chicken wings
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Buffalo Sauce

  • 1/3 cup Frank's Wings Hot Sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon waters

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Line baking sheet with alumnim foil, place wire rack, spray with non-stick
  3. Pat wings dry with paper towel, place in large bowl
  4. Put salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and baking powder in small bowl. Sprinkle seasoning over the wings, tossing to coat the wings evenly.
  5. Arrange wings on the wire rack, skin side up
  6. Place in oven, turn every 20 minutes, may take up to a hour
  7. Remove from oven, and transfer to sauce bowl, toss to cover it evenly

For Buffalo Sauce

  1. Put all Buffalo Sauce ingredients into a sauce pan, mix well until sugar dissolves
  2. Remove from heat, cool sauce to room temperature.
