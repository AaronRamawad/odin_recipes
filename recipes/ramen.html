Tonkotsu Ramen

A picture of Tonkotsu Ramen

The recipe for Tonkostu Ramen is fairly cheap, and can be found in your basic marketplaces. Ramen is orginated from Japan, where they are known for their noodles delicacies. The meal is very healthy and has all the rights things that you will need and enjoy. It will take up to 30-60 mins to cook a serving size for a bowl of Tonkotsu Ramen.

Ingredients

Stock

  • 2 pound chicken bones
  • 2 pound pork neck bones
  • 1 Pork Trotter, halved lengthwise
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 1 piece fresh ginger, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • 4-6 quarts cold water
  • 1 Kombu Sheet

Chashu Pork

  • 3 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 boneless pork shoulder
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 1 piece fresh ginger
  • 1 de arbol chile
  • 1/2 cup mirin
  • 1/2 cup sake
  • 6 tablespoons shoyu
  • 2 tablespons rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar

Pickled Mushrooms

  • 2 ounces dried shittake mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup shoyu
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 piece fresh ginger

Ramen

  • 4 to 6 budles dried ramen noodles
  • 1 cup shoyu
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons toasted seasame oil
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • 3 tablespoons canola oil
  • 4 baby bok choy
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 to 6 soft-cooked eggs
  • 4 to 6 small nori sheets
  • Chile Oil(optional)

Directions

  1. Prepare the Stock

    2. Prepare chickenbones, pork bones, trotter scallions, and ginger ina larger stockpot Cover with cold water, bring to a raod boil over high, boil for at least 6 to 16 hours. Reduce stock to 2 quarts, stirk kombu inside and cool at room temperature.

  2. Prepare the Chashu Pork

    3. Heat dutch oven with a tight-fitting lid over medium heat, add oil. Add pork and sear until golden bronw on 2 sides, add garlic scallions and ginger. Carefully add mirin, sake, and shoyu, rice wine vinegar, and brown sugar. Bast the pork every 30 minutes.

    Prepare the pickled mushrooms, bring water in medium saucepan to a boil over high. Stir in mushrooms, cover and let soak. Drain mushrooms and thinly slice. Pour rice wine vinegar, shoyu, water, and grated ginger to a boil in small pot.

  3. Prepare the Ramen

    4. Boil the water, and bring it to a soft consistency. Now add everything together and pour into bowls. Enjoy!!!
