Homemade Pizza

A picture of homemade pizza

In this recipe, you will be able to use basic ingredients tha reside in your kitchen. Its a dish that is very simple to make by yourself, or with family. All it requires is a oven, which is in most households. It is able to serve a whole family for night, or save for tasty mid-week snack.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Sea Salt
  • Extra Virigin Olive Oil

Directions

  1. Stir together the water, sugar, and yeast. Set aside for 5 minutes
  2. Place flour and salt in stand mixer, add yeast mixture, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  3. Turn dough onto flat surface, then knead it into a ball
  4. Place dough into a bowl lined with olive oil, and cover with plastic to rise
  5. Take dough of the bowl, and knead to a prefered size, place in pizza pan
  6. Put in oven at 500 farenheit and bake for 10-13 minutes
